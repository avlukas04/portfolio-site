About Me

Hi, I’m Andrea! I love building things that blend creativity with technology. I’m currently studying Computer Science and Data Science at UC Berkeley, and I’ve gotten to work on projects ranging from AI-powered building tools to web apps that mix design with generative AI. What excites me most is taking an idea, figuring out how it can actually work, and then making it real, whether that means diving into the backend, sketching out a user flow, or experimenting with a new model. Outside of work, you’ll probably find me at dance practice, lifting weights, tinkering with side projects, or chasing down a new skill just because it sounds fun.
